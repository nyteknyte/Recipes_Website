Cooked salmon

Main page

Description

Golden-crisp on top and just barely cooked in the center, this pan seared salmon is easy to make and elegant to serve

Ingredients

  • cooked salmon
  • salt
  • pepper
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Season the salmon
  2. Heat the oil in a 12-inch nonstick skillet over medium-high heat until hot and shimmering
  3. Cook the salmon, skin side up, until golden and crisp, about 4 minutes
  4. Carefully flip the fillets and reduce the heat to medium. Continue cooking until done to your liking, 4 to 5 minutes more
  5. Transfer to a platter and serve. Enjoy!
